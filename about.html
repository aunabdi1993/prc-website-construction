Raza Rubbani

Managing Director

PRC Recruitment was created to enable executives and hiring managers lend and lean on Raza’s industry experience and knowledge, in turn making hiring and resourcing a more efficient, ethical, and sustainable process for stakeholders.

Raza has 25 years’ experience within the supply chain and operations sector, in industries ranging from FMCG, retail, manufacturing, food and beverage, hospitality and engineering. Experienced in innovation, collaborative, strategic operations development. Multifaceted strategic implementation and strong passion for end-to-end completion in project management, customer centric focus and experience.

Our aim is to facilitate national, international commercial and resourcing compliance. Implementing solutions, upkeep demand, upscaling, and communicating with all teams, C-level stakeholders, key decision makers and influencers whilst assisting our partners to make effective business management decisions.
